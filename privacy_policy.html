Privacy Policy

Missing People Application (MPA) Privacy Policy is committed to securing customer information and anyone who visits our website, information, records accurate and confidentially secured. MPA Privacy policy is created and designed to ensure our commitment to meet and exceed their expectations. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the MPA application ("Application"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1.0 Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information: MPA collects certain personal information during the registration process, such as your email address, name, and optionally, your phone number.
  2. Authentication Data: To enhance the security of your stored applications, MPA may collect and store your personal passcode, password, biometric data (such as fingerprints or facial recognition data), or any other security mechanism you choose to use to unlock the Application and access your stored mobile applications.
  3. Device Information: When you use MPA, we may collect information about your device, including but not limited to device type, operating system version, and device identifier. This information is used to improve the performance and compatibility of the Application.

2.0 How We Use Your Information

  1. Application Functionality: The primary purpose of MPA is to provide you with a secure and convenient way to store, access, and manage your mobile applications. Your personal information, such as authentication data, is used solely for this purpose.
  2. Sync and Data Backup: MPA offers the option to sync your preferences and stored applications using your Google account. This helps ensure your preferences are preserved even if you switch to a new device.
  3. Security Enhancements: MPA adds an extra layer of security to sensitive applications, such as banking apps. Your chosen security mechanism is used to subscribe to Missing People Applications, helping to prevent unauthorized access.

3.0 Data Security

  1. Strong Security Controls: We are committed to maintaining the security of your personal information. We implement strong security controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your data. This includes safeguarding your chosen security mechanism, ensuring it cannot be compromised.
  2. Data Encryption: Your personal and authentication data is stored in an encrypted format to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive information.

4.0 Limitations and User Control

  1. Application Management: MPA operates independently of your device's internal applications management. You can uninstall or clear the data of your choice within MPA without affecting the overall functionality of the Application.
  2. User Control: You have the ability to control the inclusion or removal of applications within MPA through updates. Each time you wish to upload a video or remove an application, you will be required to provide authentication using your chosen security mechanism.

5.0 Uninstallation

  1. Application Uninstallation: If you choose to uninstall Missing People Application (MPA), it will not affect other applications stored on your device. They will continue to operate independently on your device.

6.0 Contact Us

  1. If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy or the way your personal information is handled, please contact us at [contact email address].

7.0 Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Please review this policy periodically for changes. Your continued use of MPA after any modifications signifies your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

By using MPA, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
