How Does MPA Works?

report a missing person

Quickly and easily report a missing person or pet. through our app. Provide essential details such as their name, age, physical description, last known location, and any other relevant information.

alert your network

Instantly alert your network of family, friends, and community members about the missing person or pet. Share updates, photos, and descriptions to spread the word and increase visibility.

coordinate search efforts

Coordinate search efforts in real-time with our integrated mapping and communication features. Collaborate with volunteers, law enforcement agencies, and search teams to cover ground efficiently.
